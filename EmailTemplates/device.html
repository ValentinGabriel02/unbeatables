Secure Your Devices & Your Images

Smart Security in a Digital World

Staying secure goes beyond strong passwords. Here’s how to keep your devices—and your online images—safe:

  • Lock Your Devices:
    Always use a PIN, password, fingerprint, or face recognition. Lock your screen when you’re away, even for a minute.
  • Update Regularly:
    Install software and security updates on your computer, phone, and tablet as soon as they’re available. Updates patch weaknesses hackers exploit.
  • Limit Access on Shared Devices:
    Avoid saving passwords or sensitive files on public or shared devices. Log out of all accounts when you’re done.
  • Be Careful with Images Online:
    Think before you share! Photos can reveal locations, faces, or personal details you didn’t intend to share. Adjust privacy settings on your social media.
  • Check Image Metadata:
    Before posting, remove hidden data (like location info) from your photos. Most phones and apps let you control this.
  • Ask Before Posting Others:
    Respect the privacy of friends, family, and colleagues. Never post someone else’s photo without their permission.
