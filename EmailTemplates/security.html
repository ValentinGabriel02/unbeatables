Your Guide to Everyday Cyber Safety

At Unbeatables, your security is our mission. Here are some practical tips to keep your digital life safe:

  • Use Strong, Unique Passwords:
    Create a password that’s long, complex, and unique for every account. Consider a password manager to keep them safe.
  • Beware of Suspicious Emails:
    Never click links or download attachments from unknown senders. When in doubt, double-check with the sender directly.
  • Protect Your Devices:
    Keep your software and antivirus up to date. Enable automatic updates for extra peace of mind.
  • Avoid Public WiFi for Sensitive Tasks:
    Don’t access sensitive accounts over public WiFi. If you must, use a trusted VPN to keep your data private.
  • Think Before You Share:
    Be cautious about what you post online. Personal information can be used for phishing or identity theft.
